Project Spotlight

St. Michael's Wedding // Event Branding

For this project, in St. Michaels, Maryland, the bride and groom wanted a formal wedding, despite the beachy location. For their invitation, we incorporated dark wood, white leather and an elegant grey and white color palette. I chose a clean sans serif font to keep things modern and sophisticated. I also did a custom monogram and illustration of the family home that was on the property.

Design in collaboration with team at Yonder Design. The copyrights to these designs belong to Yonder, but this is representative of projects I worked on while employed there.
